Peer Tutor Reflection

Being a peer tutor has been a truly transformative experience. Not only did I support others in their academic growth, but I also deepened my own understanding of how people learn differently. I learned to adapt my communication and use strategies like modeling, positive reinforcement, and collaborative goal setting. This role taught me patience, empathy, and how to celebrate small victories—both mine and my peers’. I now recognize that learning is not just about the content, but about the connection, encouragement, and trust built between people.

— Peer Tutor
