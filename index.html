Peer Tutoring Reflection

General Reflection

Throughout this peer tutoring journey, I developed both my subject mastery and interpersonal communication skills. Each session presented unique challenges and learning moments, helping me grow as both a tutor and a learner. Below are snapshots from this experience, each with a meaningful story behind it.

First Tutoring Session

This photo captures the first tutoring session I led. I was nervous but excited. Despite initial jitters, the student and I quickly connected, and I found confidence in explaining complex math concepts.

Explaining a Tricky Concept

Here, I was using analogies to explain a challenging physics concept. Watching the student's eyes light up when they understood was incredibly rewarding and reminded me why I love teaching.

Collaborative Learning

In this session, we worked together on problem-solving rather than a one-way teaching method. This collaborative approach turned out to be more effective and enjoyable for both of us.
